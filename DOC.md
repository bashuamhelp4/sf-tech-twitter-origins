# Twitter (X) Origins – San Francisco Case Study

## Historical Background
Twitter was founded in March 2006 in San Francisco by **Jack Dorsey**, **Noah Glass**, **Biz Stone**, and **Evan Williams**. The service, originally called *twttr*, launched publicly on **July 15 2006**. It grew rapidly, becoming a key platform for micro‑blogging and public conversation. In 2023 the company was rebranded as **X** under Elon Musk’s ownership.
